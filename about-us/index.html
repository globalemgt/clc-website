About Us

POWERFUL LIGHTING. FULL CONTROL.

Controlled Lighting is a team of passionate innovators who specialize in optimizing commercial building efficiencies through the application of LED Lighting and the Internet of Things (IoT).

Our careful approach to each project and deep knowledge of the industries we serve is why we are trusted by some of the most established institutions in the United States.

A GLOBAL LEADER IN THE APPLICATION OF LED LIGHTING AND SMART WIRELESS CONTROLS.

We have an outstanding 12+ year track record of working with our partners and clients in commercial, industrial, healthcare and education spaces. As a Service Disabled Veteran Owned Small Business (SDVOSB), we are also proud to deliver BAA & TAA compliant solutions to Federal and State clients.

Our focus is to reduce energy consumption and increase operational productivity through the integration of intelligent LED lighting and wireless control systems.

BEYOND LIGHTING

We are proud to offer a complete suite of Future-Proof Smart Building solutions with our range of industry-leading technology partners.
