MaxLite C-Max RF Lighting Controls

Plug-and-Play Platform With Multi-Tier Control

C-Max by MaxLite is your go-to solution for scalable, versatile lighting controls. With plug-and-play functionality, the platform offers multi-tier control across a wide range of fixtures.

  • The integrated USB-C receptacles allow easy Luminaire Level Lighting Control (LLLC) integration across MaxLite’s extensive line of indoor and outdoor fixtures. Just pair c-Max sensors/nodes with CR fixtures and you’re set!

  • With a broad range of sensors, network nodes, and zone controllers, c-Max seamlessly adapts from small projects to large-scale installations.

From stand-alone Basic to DLC/NLC qualified Network to enterprise-level controls, c-Max saves energy, enhances occupant comfort, and ensures your facility meets the latest code standards.
