MaxLite M Series

Three Applications - One Design Solution

The M Series by MaxLite is your all-in-one outdoor lighting solution, combining a slim, clean design with the flexibility to serve as an Area, Flood, or Wall fixture based on your mounting and optic choices.

The M Series comes CCT selectable, offering 3 color temperatures in one product for added flexibility. It’s equipped with a controls-ready receptacle, allowing you to add basic controls like an on/off photocell or motion sensor, or more advanced network nodes for grouping and sophisticated control functions. Additional options include high voltage (277-480V), battery backup, visual comfort lens, backlight shield, and visors.

The M Series family delivers a complete outdoor lighting solution.
