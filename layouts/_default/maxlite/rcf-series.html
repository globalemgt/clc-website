MaxLite RCF Series

No Labor, Critical Protection

The RCF Series Universal Commercial Downlight by MaxLite is your cost-effective, all-in-one solution for recessed lighting in hospitality, retail, office, and commercial spaces.

  • Designed with an integral whip for easy connection to a junction box, it eliminates the need for traditional housings, saving you time and money.

  • This versatile downlight combines 5 CCTs and 3 wattages in one, making it perfect for new builds, remodels, and retrofits.

  • Its slim design fits shallow ceilings, while the white baffle trim reduces glare, providing soft, eye-pleasing illumination.
