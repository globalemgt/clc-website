Details

The ISON™ LDRMH1 is a multi-purpose troffer retrofit designed to upgrade existing lighting troffer fixtures in Healthcare or other facilities. This technology creates an inhospitable environment for the growth of bacteria and other pathogens.

Prevents the growth of:

  • Bacteria
  • Fungi
  • Yeast
  • Mold
  • Mildew
  • And Much More (see spec sheet for the full list)

The Antimicrobial LED spectrum comes in a White Antimicrobial Light Mode in 4000k 80 CRI that functions as normal LED fixture allowing for day to day task to be completed while reducing bacteria.

Use 'Antimicrobial+Light Mode' when personnel are in the room for continuous antimicrobial action. Or use 'Enhanced Antimicrobial Mode' when illumination not required. Enhanced Antimicrobial mode emits a higher dosage of Vital Vio's antimicrobial wavelengths to maximize antimicrobial protection. Both modes meet international standards for continuous and unrestricted use around people.

To add another layer of prevention the LDRMH1 is painted with Britex™ a white highly reflective antimicrobial coating to suppress growth of bacteria on the surface of the fixture.

  • BAA & TAA Compliant

Warranty Information

Orion ISON class LED fixtures are covered by a 10-year warranty excluding continuous disinfection white light option. Antimicrobial protection option is covered by a 5-year warranty. Accessories and individual components are covered by separate OEM supplier warranties.
